FAQ’S

1. How do I find a recipe on your website?

You can easily find a recipe by using the search bar at the top of the page. Simply type in the dish you're looking for, and we’ll provide a list of relevant recipes. Alternatively, you can browse recipes by category (e.g., Quick Meals, Desserts, Vegan, etc.).

2. Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes, by creating a free account, you can bookmark and save your favorite recipes to easily access them later.

3. How do I submit my own recipes?

Go to the “Submit Recipe” page, fill out the form with ingredients, steps, and photos, then submit for review by our team.

4. Are the recipes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Each recipe includes step-by-step instructions, and we tag beginner-friendly dishes to help you start cooking with ease.

5. How do I know if a recipe is vegan, gluten-free, or dairy-free?

Recipes include labels and icons to indicate if they meet dietary restrictions. You can also filter by dietary preference using our search.

6. Can I print recipes from the website?

Yes, each recipe page includes a print option so you can easily create a hard copy for use in your kitchen.

7. Do you offer video tutorials?

Many of our recipes include step-by-step video tutorials. You’ll find them embedded within the recipe page if available.

8. Can I share recipes with my friends and family?

Absolutely! Each recipe has built-in share buttons for WhatsApp, Facebook, and more. You can also copy the direct link.

9. How can I contact you if I have questions or suggestions?

Visit our Contact Us page to send a message or email us directly. We welcome all feedback and love hearing from users!

10. Is there a mobile app for this website?

Yes! Our app is available for download on both Android and iOS so you can access recipes on the go.
