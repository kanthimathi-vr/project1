Delicious Chicken Briyani

Ingredients

  • Plain yogurt (Indian curd)
  • Cloves,cardoman, cinnamon, star anise
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Garam masala (or biriyani masala)
  • Salt
  • Ground turmeric
  • Red chili powder (reduce for kids)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Basmati rice (aged)
  • Chicken (preferably thighs & drumsticks)
  • Biryani masala (garam masala for layering)
  • Large onions
  • Coriander leaves
  • Mint leaves

    • Step by step Procedure:

  • Clean and wash the chicken pieces. Transfer chicken to a large mixing bowl and add lemon juice. Rub the juice all over the chicken Then, add all the spices -chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala Mix and roll the chicken to coat in the marinade.
  • Heat oil in a pressure cooker.
  • Add spices- Clove, cardamon, cinnamon and star anise
  • Add onion and saute well till golden brown.Add Tomatoes,ginger garlic paste and saute well
  • Add some fresh finely chopped coriander and mint leaves.Add marinated chicken and saute for 5 mintes
  • Add required amount of water and allow it to boil.Add soaked rice and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
  • Open after cools down and serve hot Chicken briyani with onion raita.
